  1. The Soul King is the king of Soul Society, whose existence in Soul Society is as symbolic as it is absolute. The king resides in the Soul King Palace that exists in a separate, special dimension in the very core of Soul Society, which is protected by the Royal Guard. The door to this dimension is opened via the Ōken.
  2. Aizen Sōsuke is the former captain of the 5th Division in the Gotei 13 and leader of the Espadas. He single handily destroyed the Gotei 13, Yoruichi Shihōin, Kisuke Urahara, baraggon, Tier Harribel, isshin kurosaki, and soo much more. To be fair he was about to finish off ichigo before urahara sealed him. He is the master of mind games. And by the end of bleach I am sure we will see him again.
  3. Yhwach, often referred to simply as “Your Majesty” by his underlings, is the monarch of the Wandenreich and Father of the Quincy. He also possesses the designation “A”.
  4. Ichigo Kurosaki is the main character of the show, so if you don’t know him then you are lost.
  5. Royal Guards is an organization that is dedicated to the protection of the Soul King, the Royal Family, and the Soul King Palace. We know that they are powerful, we just have not seen much from any of them,so again the ranks will more than likely change.
  6. Genryūsai Shigekuni Yamamoto was the captain of the 1st Division in the Gotei 13 and also the Captain-Commander. In my option he has the best and most powerful bankai. His death brought great sadness to the fans of bleach. And he will be truly missed.

  10. Shunsui is the captain of the 1st Division and Captain-Commander of the Gotei 13. His lieutenants are Nanao Ise and Genshirō Okikiba. He is the former captain of the 8th Division. Jushiro is the captain of the 13th Division in the Gotei 13. His lieutenant is the lovely Rukia Kuchiki. They are the two strongest captains in bleach.   11. Kisuke Urahara is the former captain of the 12th Division, as well as the founder and 1st President of the Shinigami Research and Development Institute. Altho he is far weaker then aizen he was the one to seal him away.
  12. Unohana Retsu formerly known as Yachiru Unohana was the captain of the Fourth Division in the Gotei 13. She was one of the oldest and most experienced captains, and was the Soul Society’s best healer, as well as the first person to wield the name Kenpachi.[2] Her lieutenant was Isane Kotetsu.
  13. Yoruichi Shihōin is the former captain of the 2nd Division of the Gotei 13, as well as the former commander of the Onmitsukidō. Having abandoned her command of both positions, she works with Kisuke Urahara and Tessai Tsukabishi, based in the Urahara Shop out in the Human World. She is one of the fastest if not the fastest characters in bleach. She took down the (0 or 10th espada) Yammy in seconds. Her only real defeat came at the hand of aizen.
  14. Coyote Starrk was an Arrancar and the Primera (1st) Espada in Sosuke Aizen’s army, along with his other half, Lilynette Gingerback. Starrk is a lazy and unenthusiastic man who spends much of his time asleep, and no one, except Lilynette Gingerback, his other half, appears to try and stop him from doing so. He is so powerful millions of hollow die just by being close to him. With victories over former Captains Love and Rose without much trouble he is truly deserving of the number on spot. Altho he fell to the current head commender shunsui he never seemed to be in the fight. And we never saw his true potential.

  20. Bambietta Basterbine is Stern Ritter E of the Vandenreich. Bambietta has the ability to turn anything that her Reishi and Reiatsu touch into a bomb. Because of this, her explosive attacks cannot be blocked. However, her attacks can be deflected before the explosion occurs, if hit by a sufficient force. With victories over 7th Division Captain Sajin Komamura and members of his squad. She is a force to be reckon with.
