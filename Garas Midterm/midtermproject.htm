Bleach is a story about people called "Shinigami" who hunt down ghosts to bring them back to the ghost realm where ghosts who need to rest go for eternal slumber. The main character, Ichigo, is a normal student until a Shinigami is forced to give her powers to Ichigo. This brings Ichigo, into the supernatural world.
