The types of characters in Bleach (Ichigo is every type)

Normal Human Hollow Shinigami Quincy Fullbringer Vizard
These are just normal humans with no powers. These are souls that are dead and are haunting Earth in unrest. These are souls, usually without a human body, that kill the evil in Hollows to save them and send them to "Heaven." These are humans with abilities to kill Hollows, but they destroy the soul, not save it. These are humans who can use items and bonds with those items to gain extra powers. These are Shinigamis who can control the evil in their heart to gain extra Hollow powers.
