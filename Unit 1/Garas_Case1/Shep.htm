Shep's Old-Fashioned Ice Cream Parlour What We Do

Shep's Ice Cream has been at the same site for more than one hundred years. Located in the small town of Hefferton, Vermont, people from all over New England come to Shep's Ice Cream Parlour. You must drop by and try our dream supreme ice cream.
