SKRILLEX

And Why He Makes The Best EDM

Skrillex is my favorite EDM (Electronic Dance Music) maker. His music is what
brought me into mixtaping and "DJing." I was intrigued at how his music represented
human instincts and emotions without a lot of human singing. I would say that it was
like showing us how robots feel emotion. I made four songs on GarageBand, and I
would say that I was inspired by his way of making music.

Skrillex's Accomplishments

  1. He recorded two albums in 2004.
  2. He began solo career in 2007.
  3. He created the Gypsehook EP in 2009.
  4. Created the "My Name Is Skrillex EP."
  5. Released the "Scary Monster and Nice Sprites" EP in late 2010.
  6. Released "More Scary Monsters and Nice Sprites" EP in mid-2011.
  7. October 30, 2011 he was nominated for five Grammy Awards at the 54th Grammy Awards.
  8. December 5, 2011, BBC announced he had been nominated for their Sound of 2012 poll.
  9. December 12, 2011, he was named MTV's Electronic Dance Music Artist of the Year.

Skrillex's Top Five Songs

  • Bangarang
  • First of the Year (Equinox)
