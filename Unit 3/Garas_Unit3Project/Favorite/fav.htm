I've always liked Charizard, from Pokemon, because his design is so simple and yet so powerful and regal looking. He is a generation 1 Pokemon, which means he was one of the first Pokemon. Charizard evolves from Charmeleon, who evolves from Charmander. Charizard has two "Mega" forms (pictured left). He is a Fire/Flying type, meaning that he is great against Grass, Ice, Bug, Fighting and Steel types, but he is weak to Rock, Water, and Electric Types.
