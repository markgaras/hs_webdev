Crit (Critical)
The word "Critical" appears when a Pokemon has an attack that has extra damage, and players eventually shortened it to a "crit."
Ev's (Effort Points)
This is an amount of seemingly random numbers that a Pokemon gains after it wins a match, and they are almost always called "Ev's."
Exp (Experience Points)
Every time a Pokemon wins a battle, it wins "experience points" that go towards leveling up, and they are just called "Exp."
Iv's (Individual Values)
When a Pokemon beats another Pokemon, it gains "individual point' that say how much the Pokemon is going to get stronger after leveling up, and they are just called "Iv's."
One Hit Knock-Out (1-hit KO)
When a Pokemon faints after only 1 hit, it is called a One Hit Knock-Out, but players just call it a "1-hit KO."
