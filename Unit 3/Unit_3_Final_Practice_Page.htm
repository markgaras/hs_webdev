My Autobiography

Mark B. Garas


     I was born on November 1, 2000, in El-Qantarah el-Sharqiyya, Egypt. I moved to York in 2004, right after my sister was born. I am currently living with my mom and dad, who are both from Egypt. My dad is from El-Qantarah el-Sharqiyya, but my mom is from Girga, Egypt. They met around 1998, and they married in 2000. I like playing basketball and videogames. I am 15, and I know English (obviously), Arabic, and I'm learning Spanish.








The Emoji That Represents Me



     I've always thought that dragons were the coolest. There are so many cool possibilities of types, and THEY BREATHE FIRE. What else can I really say? They can have armored skin, wings, be in any color, etc. I think that they are my favorites because of an old Japanese story of how a really weak fish swam up a river and turned into a dragon. I think that this is a beautiful story.
